Welcome

The Sukham Saarthi Foundation is a non-profit organization based in Vadodara, Gujarat, established on August 8, 2023. It focuses on a variety of social issues including health and family welfare, children's education and literacy, human rights, labor and employment, and women's development and empowerment. The foundation operates as a private sector company under the Companies Act, 2013.

The foundation provides healthcare services, educational support, legal aid for human rights protection, employment training programs, and initiatives to empower women.
